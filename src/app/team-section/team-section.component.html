Antonetta Njeri Kariru

Director

Antoneta Njeri Kariru is a Lecturer and Consultant in Hotel and Hospitality Management. Antoneta has over ten years experience lecturing Hospitality Management in several universities in Kenya. She holds a Doctor of Philosophy - PhD in Hospitality Management, Master of Science - MSc in Hospitality Management and Bachelor of Science- BSc in Hotel and Institution Management (First Class Honours), all from Maseno University, Kenya. Due to the wide experience and exposure to Hospitality Management, Antoneta has developed exceptional skills in Hospitality Management, Hospitality Training, Hospitality Research and Hospitality Consultancy.
